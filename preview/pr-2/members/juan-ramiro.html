Juan Ramiro

Juan Ramiro is the AI Research Manager within Ericsson Cognitive Software Engineering, where he leads R&D activities with mid and long-term horizon, involving exploration of new technologies, forward-looking concepts and disruptive network optimization methodologies. With over 15 years of experience in the telecom industry, he has held positions related to R&D, strategy, marketing and technical sales. Juan holds a Telecom Engineering degree from Malaga University, a PhD in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Aalborg University, an Executive MBA from San Telmo Business School and an Executive Degree in Big Data & Business Analytics from EOI. He is also an Honorary Associate Professor at Malaga University, as well as the co-author of a book on Self-Organizing Networks
