Framework-Agnostic Libraries are needed
Lucia Ferrer Experienced Data Scientist

Deep learning has grown fast, really fast. It’s now a major part of how companies make decisions and design products. With this boom, AI has become more powerful, but also way more complex. One big challenge? The explosion of tools and frameworks. We’re now living in a world full layers of complexity to build on top of backends1. Diverse frameworks are coexisting such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, JAX, MXNet, and more, each with its own quirks and tradeoffs.
